About Us (Global India Security Services)

Global India Security Services (GISS) holding an impeccable track record in providing security manpower services from a decade and over, and with the same humongous work experience we spread our wings in Pan India basis providing services for various organizations.

The organization has been established under the leadership of Mr. Subhash Chand Sharma in the year 2014. Global India Security Services (GISS) is a premier provider of innovative and comprehensive security services, designed to address the dynamic challenges of today’s world. Specializing in a wide range of offerings, including physical security, surveillance systems, and cybersecurity, GISS ensures the safety of businesses, institutions, and individuals. Their client-centric approach emphasizes preventive measures, real-time responsiveness, and tailored strategies that align with the specific needs of each client.
